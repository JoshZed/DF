Drink'N'Fit

Drink'N Fit is a responsive fitness application that allows individuals to easily engage in fitness activities no matter where they are. Drink'N Fit uses the habitual symbol of alcohol to sway individuals into healthy activity by detouring the idea that you need to give up on all your vices in order to peruse healthy activity.
